A digital cartographic explorer made for the right honorable coder wishing to incorporate historical map tiles into their own product

Use the search tool, atlas, and map list to find a specific map. Once a map is selected, the historic tiles will appear in the main map window and the metadata sidebar will show. The code snippet that enables use of those tiles will be at the bottom of the metadata sidebar.
